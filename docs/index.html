Core Features

⚔

Auto-Attack Combat

Your weapons automatically target and attack nearby enemies. Focus on movement and survival while your arsenal does the damage.

🌟

Roguelike Progression

Collect XP gems, level up, and choose from random upgrades each level. Every run is unique with different build paths.

⚙

Weapon Evolution

12 unique weapons that can evolve into powerful awakened forms. Combine max-level weapons with passive items to unlock ultimate abilities.

🌎

Procedural World

Explore a vast 4000x4000 procedurally generated desert filled with ancient ruins, cacti, and mysterious landmarks.

💰

Meta Progression

Earn gold to buy permanent upgrades at the merchant. Unlock new characters and abilities that persist between runs.

🎨

Pixel Art Style

Beautiful AI-generated 16-bit pixel art with animated characters, enemies, and environments. Desert aesthetics with Arabian mythology.
